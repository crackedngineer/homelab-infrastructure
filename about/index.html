About

I’m a Software Engineer focused on building scalable backend systems, AI-powered applications, and platform infrastructure. My work sits at the intersection of backend engineering, DevOps, and automation — where reliability, performance, and developer experience matter.

I enjoy working on low-latency systems and solving complex problems around distributed architectures, data pipelines, and system design. Recently, I’ve been exploring AI agents and how they can be integrated into real-world applications to automate workflows end-to-end.

Some areas I actively work on:

  • Backend development using Python and Golang
  • Designing microservices and scalable APIs
  • Building and optimizing data pipelines (Kafka, async systems)
  • Kubernetes, containerization, and platform engineering
  • CI/CD, GitOps, and infrastructure automation
  • AI integrations and developer tooling

I’ve worked on platform teams where I contributed to internal developer platforms, automated infrastructure provisioning, and reduced operational overhead through smart tooling and workflows.

Outside of work, I build side projects around AI and developer productivity, and maintain a homelab where I experiment with Kubernetes, GitOps, and production-like infrastructure setups.

I’m always interested in roles where I can work on scalable systems, platform engineering, or AI-driven products — especially in domains like fintech, developer tools, or high-performance systems.
